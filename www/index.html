FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How are glass fabricators, contractors and carpenters enrolled in the Ozone Ozostars program?

    Glass fabricators, contractors and carpenters can be enrolled in the Ozone OzoStars program in the following ways:
    Channel Partner (Dealer/Retailer) - WAP Link for Glass Fabricator, Contractor and Carpenter Enrollment
    > Ozone Sales Team Login App/WAP Link
    > Self sign-up through OzoStars app
    Ozone Helpdesk Support - Glass fabricator, contractor and carpenter like you need to submit details like name, mobile number, member type, ID, proof, bank canceled check copy and PAN card. Once the registration is verified, you will receive an automatic welcome SMS with your login ID and an app link. Ozone Overseas reserves the right to reject/cancel membership on behalf of the Company.

  • How can a newly enrolled member login to Ozone OzoStars App?

    Newly enrolled members like you can login to Ozone OzoStars app through your registered mobile number and using OTP sent to your registered mobile number. Note, if this is your first login, you will remain logged in to the app until you manually log out of the account.

  • How can a member submit product codes to earn loyalty points?

    By scanning QR codes on original Ozone products through Ozone OzoStars, a member earns Loyalty Reward Points.

  • What will happen to your earned points?

    You will get rewards in terms of Vouchers, or gifts which is available in Ozostars Redemption Window.

  • Are there tears in ozone Ozostars?

    Yes, there are total 4 tiers in the program - Silver Star, Gold Star, Super Star and Mega Star and each tier has tier wise fixed points rewarding process.

  • How can I help registered members to redeem their points?

    Points deposited by any registered member will be converted into Vouchers or gifts option available in Ozostars application. 80% of his total earned point he can redeem any time rest 20% points he can redeem only annually. (Financial year to be followed). Member's account must have at least 2500 redeemable points for Vouchers.

  • Where can I download Ozone OzoStars App?

    You can download Ozone OzoStars app from Google Play Store (for Android users) and Apple App Store (for iOS users) and sign in through your registered mobile number.

  • What is the validity of my points earned in the program?

    All points earned in the program have a validity of 12 months, and they expire after 3 months in the following year. For example: Points earned during the period 1st January to 31st March will expire on 30th April of the following year.

  • Whom should I contact to resolve any program-related matter?

    For any program related issue you can contact your Ozone Sales Officer or by calling Ozone Program Help Desk (Toll Free) number 18002020204. You can also email us at email id: loyalty@ozone-india.com.

  • How do we check whether the ozone product is original or not?

    You can check the originality of any ozone product by scanning the fat code on the ozone product. Once the fat code is scanned, you will receive a message that says "Thank you for purchasing the original Ozone product".

  • How to register product warranty online?

    A product warranty can be registered online through 3 steps:
    > By scanning the QR code marked on the Ozone product
    > Click on the Warranty Registration tab, fill in the required details and upload the product invoice
    > Please download the warranty card
